What We Offer

Advanced Procedures

Explore the range of specialized dental procedures available at our clinic.

Dental Implants

Titanium implants surgically placed into the jawbone to permanently replace missing teeth. They function like natural teeth, offering long-lasting support for crowns, bridges, or dentures with excellent aesthetics and durability.

Disimpaction

Surgical removal of impacted teeth — especially wisdom teeth — that are trapped beneath the gum or growing at abnormal angles. This prevents infection, crowding, and damage to adjacent teeth.

Root Canal Treatment

Endodontic therapy to save severely infected or damaged teeth. The infected pulp is carefully removed, the canal is cleaned and sealed, and the tooth is restored with a crown for long-term functionality.

Jaw Surgery

Orthognathic surgery to correct jaw irregularities, misalignment, and skeletal discrepancies. This procedure improves bite function, facial symmetry, and can resolve issues like TMJ disorders.

Prosthetic Rehabilitation

Complete oral rehabilitation through custom-designed prosthetics — including full and partial dentures, fixed bridges, and implant-supported restorations — restoring form, function, and confidence.
